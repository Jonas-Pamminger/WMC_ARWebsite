Augmented Reality

We are three students of the HTL-Leonding. Our website is about the Augmented Reality. We are trying to make structured Documents which match the 3D-model-Background. Our Goal is that we:

  • Let users watch 3D modules in the web
  • A site where you can find out everything about AR
  • Expanded the real world with our website
  • We also what the try to cooperate with real companies

But it is still hard to work through these goals. There are still some Risks like:

  • There is to less computing power for our project
  • It's too difficult to code it from scratch
  • That we don't have enough time to finish it

But there are some Deadlines that we are going do for sure. Those Deadlines are:

  • Nice frontend
  • The text for our information page
  • Make a 3D models viewer in the web on a black background
  • Try to get our 3D models in the real world

But also a good presentation which u can see at the end of the second Semester

The static part is following these Units:

  • What's the different between AR and VR?
  • How can someone use AR and VR?
  • The history of AR and VR
  • The risks of AR and VR

There is also a Dynamic part about our project that:

  • find a page where u can find the 3d models

  • you can choose which objects should be in 3d
